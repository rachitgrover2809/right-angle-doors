Our Philosophy

Made in Jaipur.
Built to Last Lifetimes.

The Right Angle has been crafting bespoke interiors and furniture in Jaipur since 1999. Our door collection is the result of that same manufacturing rigour applied to the single most visible element of any interior — the door.

Solid Core Construction
Every door is built on a solid or engineered core — never hollow. Weight and resonance matter.
Real Wood Veneers
Walnut, oak, teak — natural veneers with matched grain, not printed laminates.
Brass & Gold Inlays
All metal inlays are solid brass, hand-fitted and precision-routed into the surface.
Custom to the Millimetre
Every door is made-to-measure. Non-standard heights, widths, and configurations are our norm.
